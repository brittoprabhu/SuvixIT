Why engineers enjoy working here

We run lean, trust experts, and keep meetings light. Engineers partner closely with product and design from idea to launch.

  • Modern stack. ASP.NET Core microservices, React frontends, automated CI/CD, and observability baked in.
  • Learning budget. Annual allowance for courses, books, or certifications aligned to your growth path.
  • Wellness first. Comprehensive health insurance for you and dependents plus no-questions-asked mental health days.

How we hire

  1. Share your resume, GitHub or project links, and a short note on what excites you.
  2. Initial video chat to learn about your goals and discuss the role.
  3. Collaborative technical exercise focused on real-world scenarios.
  4. Meet future teammates to explore culture fit and ways of working.
  5. Receive offer with transparent compensation and onboarding plan.
